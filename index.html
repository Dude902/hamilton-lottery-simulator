Hamilton Lottery Simulator

This simulator assumes 10 pairs of tickets available each night with 50,000 people entering each lottery.

For each entry you have a 1 in 5,000 chance of winning.

If you enter 3,500 times, you have a ~50% chance of winning at least once.

If you enter 23,000 times, you have a ~99% chance of winning at least once.

It is estimated that this simulator will take around 6 and 1/2 minutes for you to win.
